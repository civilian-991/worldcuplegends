## Design Guidelines

### Color Palette
- **Primary Gold:** #D4AF37
- **Dark Background:** #0A0A0A
- **Night Blue:** #1A1A2E
- **White:** #FFFFFF
- **Gold Gradient:** linear-gradient(135deg, #D4AF37 0%, #C5A028 100%)

### Style Notes
1. **Products:** Clean, professional e-commerce style. Dark backgrounds work best.
2. **Legends:** Dramatic, heroic poses. High contrast, stadium lighting feel.
3. **Banners:** Cinematic, epic sports imagery. Can include stadium/crowd elements.
4. **Overall Mood:** Premium, luxurious, celebratory of football heritage.
